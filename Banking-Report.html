WHY (Algorithmic & Architecture Justifications)
Why Random Forest over Logistic Regression?

Logistic Regression models collapsed into an AUC of 0.479 (worse than baseline noise tracking). Random Forest successfully structures localized non-linear conditions separating Debt-to-Income metrics and historical indicators that linear transformations cannot segment smoothly.

Why maintain Raw PD metrics over raw ML Classification outputs?

With a strict baseline of 80 training matrix coordinates, standard validation lines are highly data-starved. Raw algorithmic PD scales are reliable and mathematically deterministic; hence, risk rules handle primary filtering while ML parses downstream complex outliers.

Why implement Unsupervised Isolation Forests?

The portfolio contains no verified downstream default labels or fraudulent classifications. Isolation Forest handles this constraint natively via multi-space path isolating trees, pinpointing mathematical outliers without upfront pattern parameters.
